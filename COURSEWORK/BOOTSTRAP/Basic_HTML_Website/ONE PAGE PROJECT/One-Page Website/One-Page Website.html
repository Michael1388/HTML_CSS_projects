Chocolate Sunflower

The Chocolate Sunflower is prized for it's beauty and showiness, it is also the rarest sunflower. It has deep burgundy petals with a chocolate-brown center.

Gallery

Its blooms are 6-8 inches wide, with deep burgundy petals and a chocolate-brown center.

It blooms from mid-summer to early fall and prefers full sun.



The chocolate sunflower grows to a height of 4-5 feet tall, with a spread of 1-2 feet.
