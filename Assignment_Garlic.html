Next we will talk about Dogs and whether they may dig up your garlic and whether garlic is good or bad. There is a lot of controversy surrounding the topic.

Can dogs eat garlic? The answer, emphatically, is no. Is It Safe for Dogs to Eat Garlic? Garlic might be good for people, but dogs metabolize certain foods differently than we do. According to the Merck Veterinary Manual, garlic and other members of the allium family, including onions, contain thiosulfate, which is toxic to dogs but not to humans.
Also, what about cats, can they eat garlic? This answer is also an emphatic no.
